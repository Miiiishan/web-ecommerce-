Our Mission

To provide a Christ-centered education for character formation, academic competence, scholarship, and community service for God’s glory.

Our Vision

To become a premier Christian educational institution in the Philippines by the year 2030.

Who We Are

Fellowship Baptist College is committed to providing quality Christian education that nurtures character, develops academic excellence, and inspires servant leadership. We aim to shape individuals who are academically competent, morally grounded, and committed to serving both God and the community. Through faith, discipline, and excellence, Fellowship Baptist College continues to provide an environment where students can grow spiritually, intellectually, and socially.
